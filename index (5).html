    How the binding works

    First

    Gather the pages

    Drop in every PDF you want combined. Each one gets a quick preview, page count, and size.

    Then

    Set the order

    Drag cards to reorder, rotate stray pages, or duplicate a cover — exactly the sequence you want bound.

    Finally

    Bind, in your browser

    Folio stitches the document together on your device. Nothing is uploaded — your files never touch a server.

    Folio runs entirely in your browser using pdf-lib and PDF.js. No file ever leaves your device.

    Built for paper-pushers, students, and anyone with too many PDFs.
